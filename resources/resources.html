The Products of Our Illustrious Students Over The Years

Students build the units website

By The Second Cohort of EIU students

In an inspiring display of innovation and collaboration, the students of the Education Unit has successfully designed and launched a new website for the institution's Education Unit. The project, which was part of a hands-on learning initiative, allowed students to apply their skills in web development, graphic design, and content creation to create a user-friendly platform for faculty and students. The website features streamlined access to resources, event updates, and academic tools, earning praise from both staff and peers. This initiative not only highlights the students' technical prowess but also underscores the importance of integrating real-world projects into education to foster practical skills and creativity.
